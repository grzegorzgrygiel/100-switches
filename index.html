100 switches puzzle

Each time you press the switch you turn the light in each of the 100 rooms on or off. The 1st time you flip the switch, all the lights are turned on. The 2nd time - in every 2nd room. The 3rd time - in every 3rd room, and so on.

The question: how many lights will remain on after you flip the switch for the 100th time?
